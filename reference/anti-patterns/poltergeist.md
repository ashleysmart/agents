# Poltergeist

A class that exists solely to pass data through to another class, with no logic or responsibility of its own.

**Applies to**: Object-Oriented Design

---

**What it is**: A class that exists solely to pass data through to another
class, with no logic or responsibility of its own.

**Why not**: Adds indirection without value. Increases the number of
concepts a reader must hold in mind. Inline it or delete it.
