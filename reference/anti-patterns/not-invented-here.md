# Not Invented Here (NIH)

Refusing to use existing, well-tested solutions and re-implementing them internally out of preference for home-grown code.

**Applies to**: Software Design

---

**What it is**: Refusing to use existing, well-tested solutions and
re-implementing them internally out of preference for home-grown code.

**Why not**: The internal implementation carries bugs, lacks tests, and
must be maintained indefinitely. Existing solutions have accumulated fixes
from real-world use. Use them.
